Elemental Attack

by Danny Chan

Atoms Demand Revenge!

A bunch of atoms are displeased that you didn't pay attention in chemistry class, so a rogue faction has set out to teach you a (chemistry) lesson.

Atoms have a valence shell containing a certain amount of electrons. Defeat each atom by fulfilling the octet rule (i.e. give it 8 valence electrons).

Defend yourself with two weapons: an ioniser and an electron gun.

The ioniser uses the perfect amount of ionisation energy to remove one valence electron.

Shoot atoms with the electron gun to fill their valence shell. Careful: you have a limited amount of electrons, but each successful hit from the ioniser will refill the electron gun.

Atoms with positive oxidation states are immune to electrons. Atoms with negative oxidation states are immune to the ioniser.

(Hint: the oxidation state tells you how many electrons you must add or remove to defeat an atom.)

Controls

Use the LEFT or RIGHT arrow keys to move. Press Z to use the ioniser. Press X to use the electron gun. Click the periodic table button to pause the game and look at the elements' weaknesses.
